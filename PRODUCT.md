# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Customers in and around Jacksonville, Arkansas deciding what to eat, checking the menu, or finding the truck on a phone.

## Purpose

Make The Factory’s menu, permanent location, walk-up pickup model, and operating hours immediately understandable. Convert interest into an in-person visit without implying that online ordering is currently available.

## Personality

Bold, mechanical, hungry, and local. The voice is direct and energetic.

## Principles

1. Lead with real food.
2. Keep the full menu readable on a phone.
3. Preserve the existing black, white, and red identity.
4. State pickup, hours, and sellout conditions plainly.
5. Keep the DoorDash connection dormant until a real storefront URL exists.
